  • Student Alumni Cell - DTU

    The Students' Alumni Cell, Delhi Technological University is a voluntary student body working under the guidance of the Dean of Alumni Affairs. Its main motive is to bridge the gap between Delhi Technological University and its esteemed alumni community.

    The Students' Alumni Cell strives to strengthen the relations of the alumni of this Institute and their alma mater.



  • Delhi Technological University

    Delhi Technological University (DTU), formerly known as Delhi College of Engineering, is an engineering university located in New Delhi, India. It is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India and Delhi's first engineering college. It was established in 1941 as Delhi Polytechnic and was under the control of the Government of India.

    It offers courses towards Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech), Master of Technology (M.Tech), Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) and Master of Business Administration (M.B.A) and contains fourteen academic departments with a strong emphasis on scientific and technological education and research.
