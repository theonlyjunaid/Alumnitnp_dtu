Student Alumni Affairs

Student's Alumni Affairs, DTU, is a student body that was formed with the aim to brigde the gap between the alumni and current students/University.This body will be responsible for conduction of all the events related to alumni and students in the University.It will also act as the Alma mater's representative for the alumni that will be available to help them out for any kind of academic/administrative work in the University.
