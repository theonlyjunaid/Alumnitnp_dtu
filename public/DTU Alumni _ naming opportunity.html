Naming Opportunities

Build a lasting legacy

Match your passions and dreams with the DELHI TECHNOLOGICAL UNIVERSITY plans for the future by making a named gift donation.

Create a legacy for yourself, a loved one, a colleague, your company or your organization.

A named gift donation is special. It requires a significant commitment and it provides significant rewards. By making a named gift you will be creating a long-lasting reminder of your commitment to the University for all to see and celebrate, now and in the future.

IF YOU ARE INTERESTED IN A NAMED FUNDING OPPORTUNITY. CONTACT US.

There are many naming opportunities to select from.

  1. Invest in places

    New Buildings:
    Create a living symbol of your commitment to excellence. Gifts begin at 50 per cent of the total cost of renovation or construction.
    Existing Buildings:
    Help develop new teaching, research or student areas at the University. Gifts should have a significant impact on the programs resident in the existing building, or cover at least 50 per cent of renovations.
    Other Architectural Features:
    Make a unique gift to the University--a fountain, ornamental buildings, landscaping, trees, benches or other similar features. Gifts should cover the cost of the project and a maintenance fund.

  2. Invest in faculty

    Named Departments, Centres & Institutes (Minimum endowment):
    Help an academic unit at DTU to achieve a new level of excellence. Your gift will lead to new successes in research and teaching.
    Named Chairs (Minimum 50% of total costing endowment):
    Named Professorships:
    Invest in exceptional scholars and researchers by creating an endowed chair or professorship. Help DTU to attract and retain the best faculty.
    Named Visiting Lecture Series:
    Bring outstanding national and international scholars to the University to share their knowledge and experience.

  3. Invest in students

    Named Scholarships/Bursaries or Named Prizes/Awards:
    Make a difference in the life of gifted and deserving students with a named academic award. You'll help the university attract top local, national and international students.
    Named Annual Funds-Scholarships or Named Annual Funds-Prizes:
    Named Annual Funds provide yearly support for a scholarship or prize. Donations will be distributed the same fiscal year the gift is provided. A multi-year commitment is required.
