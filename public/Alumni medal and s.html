Medal and Scholarship

DTU invites Individuals, Societies & Foundations to institute Medals & Scholarship to inspire the students. In this regard, DTU has crafted guidelines for initiating such medals and scholarships. Student shall be awarded only one scholarship/ medal. In case student is found to be eligible for more then one scholarship/ medal, he/she shall be awarded higher amount scholarship/medal and the other shall be awarded to next eligible student.


The criteria for award of Medals/Scholarship can be decided by the donor, however an indicative list is given below:

  1. Overall topper in the University.
  2. Overall topper of a particular year/semester.
  3. Topper of a branch overall.
  4. Topper of a branch in a particular year.
  5. Topper of a branch in a particular year.
  6. Best sports person Male/Female.
  7. It could be also for a particular category of student viz. general/ reserved/ PWD.

SCHOLARSHIP

The guiding principles for instituting Medals/Awards/Scholarship at DTU:
  1. Medal shall be preferably of 100 gms silver with gold plating.
  2. The medal shall have DTU logo on one side and donor’s inscription on the reverse side.
  3. The medalist shall receive medal and citation at the time of convocation or any date decided by the university along with a cash incentive of 40,000.
  4. The Cheque/Draft for institution of Medals & Scholarship may be issued in the favour of "DTU Medals & Scholarship".
